United Kingdom

I had the privilege of visiting the U.K. on two ocasions: in 2018 and 2021. These are some of the highlights from my time there

Loc Tay

Deep in the heart of Scotland, Loch Tay is a serene retreat surrounded by rolling hills, ancient history, and the charm of the Scottish Highlands. It’s a place where nature’s stillness invites you to slow down and soak in the magic of the landscape.

Loch Tay is one of Scotland's largest lochs, stretching over 14 miles. The shimmering waters, flanked by the towering Ben Lawers mountain range, create a postcard-perfect view at every turn. Whether you’re admiring it from the shore or gliding across its surface, the loch’s beauty is captivating.

Liverpool: The Heart of Football and Culture

Liverpool is a city that lives and breathes passion. Known for its rich history, vibrant music scene, and world-famous football clubs, it’s a place where every corner echoes with energy and pride.
